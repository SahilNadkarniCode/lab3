Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • butter

Instructions

1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
2. Cook the bacon in the skillet until crispy. Remove from skillet and set aside.
3. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
4. Place two slices of bread, butter side down, in the skillet.
5. Add a slice of cheese and some tomato slices to each sandwich.
6. Place the remaining slices of bread, butter side up, on top of the sandwiches.
7. Cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
8. Cut the sandwiches in half and serve warm, with the bacon on the side.
